Sponsors

Thank you to our sponsors for supporting GreyHat! We’re currently recruiting sponsors for 2025–2026.

Fall 2025-2026

Interested in sponsoring?

GreyHat, the student-run cybersecurity club at Georgia Institute of Technology, is seeking sponsors and supporters for the 2025–2026 school year!

This is a great opportunity to expose your organization to talented, security-minded undergraduates and our broader online community of nearly 2,000 undergrads, graduate students, and alumni.

Specifically, we are looking for sponsors to deliver talks to students, sponsor CTF problems/prizes, and fund snacks for our meetings. We are also looking for industry partners who can help mentor advanced students through semester-long projects.

Support can be given via a sponsorship or as a tax deductible gift.

Please email greyhat.gt@gmail.com for more information.
