GreyHat Club

About

GreyHat is Georgia Tech's Cybersecurity club. We focus on teaching practical skills in analyzing, exploiting, and defending computer systems, along with reverse engineering of applications. In addition, we have a competitive Capture-the-Flag (CTF) team. All skill levels are welcome, even if you've never heard the word "cybersecurity" before! We also host a yearly beginner-intermediate CTF in the fall, WRECKCTF (ctftime).
We are open to all GT students, including OMSCS students.

GreyHat holds two weekly meetings:.

- Tuesdays (6:30PM in Klaus 1443): CTF team meetings where we will solve CTF problems (What is a ctf?).

- Thursdays (6:30PM in Klaus 1443): general meeting in which members or guest lecturers present on cyber security topics

Presentations from prior years can be found in the #archives channel on Discord Join our Discord group for club communications, security chatter, CTF competitions, and GT cybersecurity course discussion!
